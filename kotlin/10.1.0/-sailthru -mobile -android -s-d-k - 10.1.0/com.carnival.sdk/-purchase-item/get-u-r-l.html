getURL

open fun getURL(): URI!

Return
URI!: The URL of the item. Note: The domain and scheme are case sensitive but paths are not. For example, if the stored URL is https://sailthru.com/Product, then Https://Sailthru.com/product would fail to match the product, but https://sailthru.com/product would.
