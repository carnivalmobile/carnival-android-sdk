trackImpression

static fun trackImpression(@NonNull sectionId: String, @Nullable urls: MutableList<URI!>?, @Nullable handler: TrackHandler?): Unit

Asynchronously registers an impression - a reasonable expectation that a user has seen a piece of content - with Sailthru SPM. Can be used in conjunction with RecyclerView.Adapter#onViewAttachedToWindow or similar to know when a particular content item has entered the user's viewport.

Parameters

sectionId - String: the Section ID on Sailthru SPM corresponding to the section being viewed. Must not be null.

urls - MutableList<URI!>?: a List of the URLs of the items contained within this section. Useful if multiple items of content are contained within a section, otherwise just pass a single-item array. Must not be null.

handler - TrackHandler?: an instance of TrackHandler. May be null, in which case onSuccess and onFailure callbacks won't be fired.
